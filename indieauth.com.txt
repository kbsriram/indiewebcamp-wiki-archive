'''<dfn>indieauth.com</dfn>''' provides two services: for developers, it handles authenticating users via [[RelMeAuth]], and users can delegate to it as their authorization server.

https://indieauth.com/totp

=== Using GPG with indieauth.com ===
You can sign in to sites with indieauth.com by signing a challenge with your GPG key! Instructions for setting up GPG auth:

https://indieauth.com/gpg
