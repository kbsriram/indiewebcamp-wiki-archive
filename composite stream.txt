A '''<dfn>composite stream</dfn>''' is a [[stream]] that interleaves multiple types of [[posts]], such as [[notes]] and [[articles]].

== IndieWeb Examples ==
The following IndieWeb community members have composite streams on their home pages:
* [[User:Tantek.com]] - http://tantek.com/
** [[notes]], [[articles]]
* [[User:Tommorris.org]] - http://tommorris.org
** [[notes]], [[articles]], [[checkins]]
* [[User:Eschnou.com]] - http://eschnou.com
** [[notes]], [[articles]], ... ?
* ...

Note: only the post types which have originals at their own site are listed.
