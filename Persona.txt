'''<dfn>Persona</dfn>''' is a protocol for [[email]]-based identity spearheaded by [[Mozilla]] that is supported by [[IndieAuth]] for [[rel-me]] links to email addresses.

See http://browserid.org/

== Criticisms ==
=== Reliance on javascript ===
Persona/browserid [https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/Persona/The_implementor_s_guide/Supporting_users_without_JavaScript relies on javascript] to work, forcing consumers to implement separate authentication functionality to support all users.
