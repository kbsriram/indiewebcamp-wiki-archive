'''<dfn>[http://news.indiewebcamp.com IndieNews]</dfn>''' is an indieweb version of [[Hacker News]] where in order to:
* submit a post - post something on your own site and send [[webmention]] 
* comment on a post - post a comment on your own site [[in-reply-to]] an IndieNews post.
* vote-up a post - sign-in with [[IndieAuth]] to enable the vote-up UI.
