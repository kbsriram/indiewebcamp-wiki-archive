''The '<dfn>[https://github.com/pfefferle/wordpress-webmention Webmention] Plugin</dfn>''' is a [[WordPress]] plugin that adds [[webmentions]] support to WordPress.

Incoming webmentions are added as comments to the original post and webmentions are sent when a post is published/updated.
