== Post Types ==
Bundle currently supports:
* '''[[articles]]''' - long form posts
* '''[[notes]]''' - short form posts


== Tools Used ==
Bundle internally uses the following tools:
* '''[[mf2py]]''' to parse microformats
* '''[[ronkyuu]]''' to send and receive webmentions
