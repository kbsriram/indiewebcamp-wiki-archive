'''<dfn>[http://ind.ie ind.ie]</dfn>''' is a website created by Aral Balkan for the Indie Tech Manifesto. The first Indie Tech platform to be developed is http://ind.ie/phone.

Despite the use of the word "indie," it is distinct from IndieWebCamp.
