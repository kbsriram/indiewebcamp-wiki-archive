'''<dfn>sleep</dfn>''' is a [[passive]] [[metrics]] [[post]] type that indicates how much time (and often a graph of how deeply) a person has slept.

== IndieWeb Examples ==
=== Aaron Parecki ===
{{aaronpk}} uses [[p3k]] to post sleep posts to aaronparecki.com since 2013(?) (need permalink to first sleep post and updated precise YYYY-MM-DD). Examples:
* http://aaronparecki.com/metrics/2014/07/28/080552/
